## Quick Summary

This package combines the best of `react-native-alarmageddon` and `expo-alarm-kit` into a single unified API:

- **Android**: Native AlarmManager (true alarm)
- **iOS 16+**: Native AlarmKit (true alarm)
- **iOS < 16**: Notifications (fallback)

**One package, one API, works everywhere.**
